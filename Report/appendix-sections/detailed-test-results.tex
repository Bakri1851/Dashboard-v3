\section{Detailed Test Results} \label{app:detailed-test-results}

This appendix records the verification of the functional and non-functional requirements of Chapter~\ref{sec:requirements}, summarised qualitatively in Section~\ref{sec:eval-functional} and Section~\ref{sec:eval-nonfunctional}. Each requirement was exercised against the deployed V2 stack through smoke and end-to-end (E2E) testing; the tables below give the test method, the outcome, and the section in which the supporting evidence is reported.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering

\hline
\textbf{ID} & \textbf{Requirement} & \textbf{Method} & \textbf{Result} & \textbf{Evidence} \\
\hline\hline
FR1 & Live ingestion of lab data & E2E & Pass & Section~\ref{sec:eval-data-ingestion}: JSON and embedded-XML parsed end-to-end into the normalised DataFrame; TTL cache invalidation and \texttt{lab\_session.json} filelock state confirmed; no data loss across runs. \\
\hline
FR2 & Computation of student struggle & E2E & Pass & Section~\ref{sec:eval-analytics-behaviour}: seven-signal scores in $[0,1]$ with four bands classifying correctly under v1 and v2; CF, clustering, IRT 2PL, BKT and improved model cross-checked against the design equations; weight redistribution verified. \\
\hline
FR3 & Computation of question difficulty & E2E & Pass & Section~\ref{sec:eval-analytics-behaviour}: five-signal baseline difficulty scores in $[0,1]$ with four bands classifying correctly under v1 and v2; the 2PL IRT difficulty model was evaluated as an alternative but did not beat the hand-set baseline and was not deployed; no silent failures. \\
\hline
FR4 & Real-time prioritisation & E2E & Pass & Section~\ref{sec:eval-instructor-features}: In-Class leaderboards ordered by score under Basic and Advanced modes; band colours track the threshold map; per-module and per-session filters update correctly. \\
\hline
FR5 & Present relevant analytics & E2E & Pass & Section~\ref{sec:eval-instructor-features}: all seven instructor views render to the design specification; displayed data cross-checked against the analytics pipeline; navigation error-free. \\
\hline
FR6 & Smart-device integration & & Scoped out & Section~\ref{sec:eval-assistant-workflow}: descoped at design time in favour of the modelling focus; restated as future work in Section~\ref{sec:eval-discussion}. \\
\hline
FR7 & Lab-assistant ranking system & E2E & Partial Pass & Section~\ref{sec:eval-assistant-workflow}: join with the six-character code; \texttt{?aid=} identity persistence; polling picks up assignments; mark-helped round-trip through the filelock; simultaneous joins safe, although the ranking system was not implemented and will be a future work. \\
