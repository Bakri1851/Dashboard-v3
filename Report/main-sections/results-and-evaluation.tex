\section{Evaluation} \label{sec:evaluation}

This chapter evaulates the dashboard system of Chapter~\ref{sec:implementation} along two dimensions, the validity of the model outputs (whether the struggle and difficulty rankings agree with observable student behaviour) and the operational behaviour of the deployed software against the responsiveness, interpretability, and robustness targets implied in Chapter~\ref{sec:design}. 
The unit of evalation is the individual weekly lab sessions as the dashboard is designed to be used within a single session and the cohort consists of approximately. For the evaluation we are going to focus on module where data was collected \textbf{25COA122}, across 23 recorded lab sessions 2 Feb 2026 to 15 May 2026. 
On majority of the weeks there were two lab sessions running back to back which were an hour each, so there are two sessions per week, except for the first and last week which had one session each.
