# Khajah, Lindsey & Mozer — How Deep is Knowledge Tracing?

Empirical comparison showing that extended BKT (with item difficulty, ability, and forgetting) matches DKT performance; grounds the decision *not* to jump to DKT and instead use interpretable extended BKT, satisfying the dashboard's interpretability requirement (NFR2). Published at EDM 2016.
