# Koren — Collaborative Filtering with Temporal Dynamics

Introduces explicit time-decay weighting in collaborative filtering to capture user/item drift; grounds the decision to weight recent activity more heavily in `A_raw` and connects the time-decay layer to the CF diagnostic layer in the analytics engine. Published at KDD 2009 and as CACM 53(4) 2010.
