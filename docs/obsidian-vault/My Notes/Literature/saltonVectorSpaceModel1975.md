# Salton, Wong & Yang — A Vector Space Model for Automatic Indexing

Canonical formulation of the vector-space model and TF-IDF term weighting; primary historical reference for the TF-IDF step in mistake clustering. Replaces the older Salton & Lesk (1968) citation as the conventional citation in modern IR textbooks.
